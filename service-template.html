VAT Consulting

UAE introduced VAT at 5% in 2018. Any business with taxable supplies above AED 375,000 per year must register with the Federal Tax Authority and file quarterly returns. We handle the entire VAT process.

Best for: Any UAE business that is VAT-registered or approaching the AED 375,000 mandatory registration threshold — including mainland, free zone, and offshore entities.
  • FTA VAT registration — mandatory and voluntary
  • Quarterly VAT return preparation and filing — accurate, on time, every period
  • VAT refund applications submitted to FTA
  • VAT health check — full review of your current VAT position
  • Voluntary disclosure — correction of past errors with minimum penalty exposure
  • Ongoing VAT advisory — input tax recovery, zero-rated supplies, and exempt transactions
⏱ VAT registration 3–5 days  |  Return filing before every quarterly deadline

How do you know which service you need?

Not every business needs every service. Here is a simple guide to help you identify what applies to your situation right now.

Company Formation

You need Company Formation if:

  • You are starting a new business in UAE and need a trade license — whether mainland, free zone, or offshore.
  • You are an existing business owner adding a new company or expanding into a new jurisdiction.
  • You are a foreign investor entering the UAE market for the first time.

PRO Services

You need PRO Services if:

  • You have employees or partners who need UAE visas, Emirates IDs, or work permits.
  • Your trade license is coming up for renewal and you want someone to handle it.
  • You have documents that need government attestation, notarisation, or translation.
Learn more →
Bookkeeping

You need Bookkeeping & Accounting if:

  • You do not have a dedicated in-house accountant and your financial records are not being maintained monthly.
  • You are approaching audit season and your books are not ready.
  • Your bank or free zone authority has asked for audited financial statements.
Learn more →
Audit & Assurance

You need Audit & Assurance if:

  • Your free zone authority requires an annual statutory audit — DMCC, IFZA, SHAMS, JAFZA, RAKEZ, and most other free zones do.
  • You are applying for a bank loan or investor funding and need clean audited accounts.
  • You are planning to sell or merge your company and need a due diligence audit.
Learn more →
VAT Consulting

You need VAT Consulting if:

  • Your business turnover is approaching or has exceeded AED 375,000 per year.
  • You are already VAT-registered but not filing returns correctly or on time.
  • You have made past VAT errors and are concerned about FTA penalties.
Learn more →
Payroll Outsourcing

You need Payroll Outsourcing if:

  • You have employees on your payroll and are responsible for WPS submissions every month.
  • You are struggling to process payroll accurately in-house or have received MOHRE warnings.
  • You want to ensure gratuity, leave, and salary calculations are correct under UAE Labour Law.
Learn more →
Customs

You need Customs Audit & Consulting if:

  • Your business imports or exports goods regularly and you are unsure if your HS codes and declared values are correct.
  • You have received a notice from the Federal Customs Authority.
  • You suspect you have been overpaying customs duties and want a review.
