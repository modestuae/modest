FAQ

Common questions about business setup in UAE

See all FAQs

Mainland companies can trade anywhere in the UAE and locally, requiring standard DED approvals. Free Zones offer 100% foreign ownership and tax exemptions but restrict trading strictly within the local UAE market without a distributor.

Depending on the jurisdiction and business activity, standard Free Zone setups take 3-5 working days, while Mainland setups can take 1-2 weeks.
