Who we are

What is Modest UAE and what do we do?

Modest UAE is a fully licensed business setup and corporate services consultancy registered with the Department of Economy and Tourism Dubai. We are a team of certified PRO officers, legal advisors, accountants, and business consultants — all specialising in the UAE regulatory environment.

What we do:

We handle the entire business journey — from your first trade license application to annual amendments, PRO services, accounting, VAT, payroll, and full company liquidation when the time comes. You get one consultancy that manages everything, so you never have to coordinate between multiple service providers.

Who we work with:

We work with first-time entrepreneurs who are setting up in UAE for the first time, experienced investors expanding into new jurisdictions, SMEs that need ongoing compliance support, and multinational companies setting up a UAE branch or subsidiary.

Where we operate:

We cover UAE mainland in Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and Sharjah — and all major free zones including JAFZA, Dubai South, DMCC, DAFZA, IFZA, SHAMS, Ajman Free Zone, and RAKEZ.
