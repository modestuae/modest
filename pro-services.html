What PRO services include

What does a PRO service cover in UAE?

PRO stands for Public Relations Officer. In UAE business terms, a PRO is a certified professional who manages all government-related tasks on behalf of your company. Here is the full scope of what our PRO team handles.

01

Visa processing — all types

We process investor visas for company owners and shareholders, employment visas for staff and managers, family and dependant visas for employees bringing their families to UAE, visit visas for business guests and corporate visitors, and visa cancellations when an employee leaves the company. Every visa type, every stage of the process — handled by our PRO team.

02

Emirates ID — new, renewal, and replacement

Emirates ID is mandatory for all UAE residents. We handle new applications for employees and investors joining UAE, renewals before expiry, and replacements for lost or damaged cards. We book appointments, accompany applicants to biometrics, and collect the completed card.

03

Trade license renewal

Your UAE trade license must be renewed annually. Missing the renewal deadline results in fines and can affect your visa processing and banking. Modest UAE manages your annual renewal — we prepare the application, submit before the deadline, pay the fees, and deliver the renewed license to you. You receive reminders well in advance so the renewal never catches you by surprise.

04

Medical fitness test

Employment visa applicants must complete a medical fitness test at an approved UAE health authority centre. We book your appointment at the nearest approved centre, provide guidance on what to bring, and collect the results for your visa application.

05

Labour card and work permit — MOHRE

Every employee on a UAE employment visa requires a labour card and work permit from the Ministry of Human Resources and Emiratisation MOHRE. We manage the complete MOHRE submission — work permit application, labour card issuance, and the Tawjeeh orientation for new workers where required.

06

Document attestation, notarisation, and translation

Many UAE processes require documents to be attested by a UAE Ministry, notarised, or officially translated into Arabic. We manage attestation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ministry of Justice notarisation, and certified Arabic translations — for educational certificates, company documents, marriage certificates, and any other document requiring official verification.

Documents required

What documents do you need for PRO services in UAE?

Standard documents for visa processing:

  • Passport copy — employee or investor, minimum 6 months validity
  • Emirates ID copy — if current UAE resident
  • Existing visa page copy — current visa status
  • Trade license copy — company's valid license
  • Passport-size photograph — white background, recent

Additional — employment visa:

  • Educational certificates — for skilled visa categories and professional roles
  • Employment contract — signed by employer and employee
  • Medical insurance certificate — mandatory for employment visa
  • NOC from previous employer — for visa transfer cases

Additional — investor visa:

  • Company MOA — attested copy
  • Share certificate — confirming ownership percentage
  • Board resolution — if applicable for corporate shareholders

Additional — document attestation:

  • Original document — educational certificate, marriage certificate, company document
  • Notarised copy where required
  • Passport copy of document holder
